Who I Am

KARTHIKEYAN A

Calibration Engineer  /  Inventor  /  Founder

I am an automotive calibration engineer with over seven years of focused experience in engine management systems, emission calibration, and chassis dynamometer testing. My work sits at the intersection of hardware, firmware, and real-world engine performance.

From the research labs at IIT Madras to production-grade calibration at VEMBSYS Technovation and Force Motors, every chapter of my career has been driven by a single goal: understanding what makes an engine perform to its absolute best.

I founded Parvath Solutions to bring that same level of precision to a wider range of R&D teams, startups, and institutions, offering not just solutions, but insight built from years of hands-on experience.
